ABOUT

Welcome to the Shaw project for education. We strive to provide educational material about language, mathematics, programming, technology, and more. Our staff are all volunteers who want to go the extra mile to provide for learners around the world.

A large part of our work revolves around augmenting Khan Academy's fantastic learning environment. We are developing an extended curriculum for their Processing.js environment, and you can check out our Khan Academy Computer Science FAQ page here. This will expand over time into a comprehensive documentation including examples and how-to's for more advanced projects.
